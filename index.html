What's a Uniq NFT?

Uniq NFTs are NFTs created on the Ultra blockchain and powered by Ultra's NFT standard. NFTs are, at their most basic, a way to define what an asset is, who owns it, and what they can do with it. If you have something digital such as a collectible, you want to be able to prove that its yours and be able to trade it. With the help of blockchain technology, NFTs allow this. They can be bought and resold on open NFT marketplaces while always tracking & proving ownership over a transparant blockchain. Learn more about Ultra's NFT standardhere.

What's a variant NFT� collection?

A variants NFT collection refers to a collection of non-fungible tokens (NFTs) that are related to each other in some way but have unique attributes or characteristics that distinguish them from each other. Using Ultra's V1 Uniq NFT standard, it will be possible to assign rarities and attributes to NFTs, allowing for NFT collections with advanced on-chain characteristics (metadata). Each distinct from other pieces in the collection, with different levels of rarity or scarcity associated with them, which can affect their perceived value in the marketplace. While Ultropia's Citizenship Passes will make use of the early V0 NFT standard, the Ultropia Citizens collection will make use of the upgraded V1 NFT standard, allowing for variant NFTs!
